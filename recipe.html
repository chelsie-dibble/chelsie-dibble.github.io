    Grilled pesto chicken sandwich

    • Time: 15 minutes
    • Serves: 1
    Ingredients Quantity
    Italian bred 2 slices
    Softened butter 1 tablespoon
    Pesto sauce 1 tablespoon
    Provolone cheese 1 slice
    Tomato 2 slices
    American cheese 1 slice

    Step 1: Cover one side of bread with butter then place it in a nonstick skillet over medium heat

    Step 2: Then place pesto sauce, provolone cheese, tomoatoes, and american cheese on the top side of the bread

    Step 3: Then place the rest of the pesto sauce on the second slice of bread, then put the two pieces of bread together

    Step 4: Gently fry the sandwich until both sides of the bead are golden and cheese is melted.
